Introduction

“𝑰𝒇 𝑰 𝒉𝒂𝒗𝒆 𝒕𝒐 𝒑𝒓𝒐𝒗𝒆 𝒕𝒉𝒂𝒕 𝑰 𝒅𝒐𝒏'𝒕 𝒍𝒊𝒗𝒆 𝒉𝒆𝒓𝒆 𝒇𝒐𝒓 𝒏𝒐𝒕𝒉𝒊𝒏𝒈... 𝑰 𝒘𝒊𝒍𝒍.”

Kayama Shimeji (烏山〆治), is a student of Hope's Peak Academy's Class 77-B, and a participant of the Killing School Trip featured in Danganronpa 2: Goodbye Despair. Her title is the Ultimate Car Racer ( 超高校級の「レーサー」lit. Super High School Level Racer )

She later becomes a member of the Future Foundation as Neo World Helper. After leaving the simulation, she joins her classmates, helping them work out their deeds.

She, along with Class 77-B, returns in Danganronpa 3: The End of Hope's Peak High School to explore their school life leading up to The Tragedy.
